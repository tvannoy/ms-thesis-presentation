Theory

Spatial Filtering

Theory

\[\sum_{m=0}^{M-1} y_m(t - \Delta_m) = M \times y(t) \]

Linear Array

\[   \Delta_m  = \frac{\left(m - \frac{M - 1}{2} \right) d}{c} \sin{(az)} \]

Rectangular Array

$$  \Delta_m = \frac{d}{c} \bigg( \left(m_y - \frac{M - 1}{2} \right) \sin{(az)} \cos{(el)} + \left(\frac{M - 1}{2} - m_z \right) \sin{(el)} \bigg) $$
